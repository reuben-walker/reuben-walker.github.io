Visualizing Watersheds with 3D Mapping

NSCC Centre of Geographic Sciences, Advanced GIS
February 2024

(Click to enlarge)

Project Description:


I used 3D mapping techniques to display watershed data on a 3D map of an area near Lunenberg, Nova Scotia. I created a Triangulated Irregular Network (TIN) from contour data, and then created a DEM, slope raster, and aspect raster from the TIN. Sub-tertiary watershed boundary data was then laid over the 3D map and compared against the different layers.
