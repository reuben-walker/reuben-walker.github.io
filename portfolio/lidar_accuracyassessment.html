Project Description:


I conducted an accuracy assessment of LiDAR data centered on the COGS campus in Lawrencetown, Nova Scotia. The above map shows the study area, with a DSM derived from the LiDAR overlaid. Additionally, the locations of the Non-Vegetated Vertical Accuracy (NVA) and Vegetated Vertical Accuracy (VVA) control points are shown on the map. The goal of the accuracy assessment was to assess whether or not the points fell within the Government of Canadaâ€™s Federal Airborne LiDAR Data Acquisition Guideline CQL1 standards.
