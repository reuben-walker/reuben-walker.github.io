Project Description:


This map is the end product of a supervised spectral classification that I conducted in PCI catalyst. I used Landsat-8 imagery of the Annapolis Valley in Nova Scotia. I identified training areas corresponding to barren, forested, urban, and herbaceous land cover, in addition to water. Then, I used spectral classification to automatically classify the rest of the area based on the spectral signatures of the training areas. I then brought the resulting raster dataset into ArcGIS Pro and created the final map.

Software Used:

  • PCI Catalyst Focus
  • Esri ArcGIS Pro
  • Google Earth Pro

Data Sources:

  • USGS Earth Explorer: Landsat-8 imagery
