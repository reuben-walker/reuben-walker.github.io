Annapolis Valley Spectral Classification



Completed for REMS 5001 - Fundamentals of Remote Sensing at NSCC Centre of Geographic Sciences in December 2023

Project Description:

This map was created from the results of a supervised spectral classification that I conducted in PCI catalyst. I identified training areas corresponding to barren, forested, urban, and herbaceous land cover, as well as water, and then used spectral classification to automatically classify the rest of the area. I then brought the resulting raster dataset into ArcGIS Pro and created the final map layout.
