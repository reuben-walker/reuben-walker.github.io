Canopy Height Model and Change Detection from LiDAR

NSCC Centre of Geographic Sciences, LiDAR Applications
April 2024

Project Description:


Using LiDAR point cloud data from GeoNOVA, I analyzed the change in vegetation height between 2007 and 2019. The area analyzed was a 3x2 kilometre area located near Halifax Stanfield International Airport. Vegetation-classified LiDAR points were compared against the ground to construct the canopy height model.
