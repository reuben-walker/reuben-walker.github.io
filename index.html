About Me

My name is Reuben Walker. I am currently a student in the Geographic Information Systems graduate certificate program at NSCC COGS, in Lawrencetown, Nova Scotia. I am originally from Vancouver, British Columbia.

In my free time, I enjoy cooking, camping, riding my bike, making electronic music, drawing, and reading comics. I also enjoy keeping shrimp in an aquarium, but I had to get rid of it when I moved. In the future, I want to have an aquarium again, as well as a vegetable garden and a couple of dogs. My dream vacation is to travel to Japan.

I'm interested in making cities in North America more walkable and bikeable, and with expanding public transit. I want to use GIS technology to help plan better cities, help expand the amount of affordable housing, and to help protect the environment.
